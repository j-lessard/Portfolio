Maine Paleoshorelines

This project began as an assignment in GIS Applications II and was an attempt to model paleoshorelines throughout Maine's history. Building upon the work of a previous student, I used the raster calculator in ArcMap and modeled individual shorelines from 10,500 BCE to 2016. Originally, the final product was a GIF animating the change over time. But I wanted to take it a step further and allow someone to, over the internet, explore the shorelines in detail year by year. In pursuit of that goal, I discovered and began learning LeafletJs. The above map is the culmination of that discovery and learning.
