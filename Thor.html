The Theater History of Operations (THOR) dataset is a monstrous dataset that describes bombing missions from WWI through the Vietnam War; it is a virtual treasure trove for anyone interested in history. Naturally, the dataset ripe for exploration. The World War II section of data was particularly appealing to me due to it's robustness and so I set about finding a suitable project. I knew that I wanted to allow people to quickly and easily explore the spatial aspect of the data, but the scope of the data quickly proved to be a challenge to many of my early concepts. Eventually, and after many failed experiments, I decided to focus in on the American missions and the nature of their aircraft and targets. After doing some data wrangling in Excel and Access, I isolated the information I was after. I used Tableau to display the data in a way that allows a user to explore the data in a way that compromises between granularity and performance.
